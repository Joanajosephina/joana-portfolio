First 90 days

How I’d approach the role from day one — learning fast, shipping early, and building a reliable cadence around experimentation.

Days 1–30

Learn & audit

  • Audit current Amplitude event taxonomy and tracking gaps
  • Map the PLG funnel and identify key drop-off points
  • Interview SMB customers to understand real workflows
  • Review existing growth learnings and channel performance
  • Identify the fastest acquisition and activation opportunities

Build & launch

  • Ship the first wave of prioritised experiments
  • Launch initial SEO / PLG acquisition bets
  • Deploy the first monetisation and upsell experiments
  • Build an activation dashboard in Amplitude
  • Establish a weekly experiment review cadence
Days 61–90

Scale winners

  • Double down on the top-performing experiments
  • Launch a referral or reseller growth loop
  • Explore Shopify / WooCommerce integration leverage
  • Present the first full growth readout with attribution context
  • Build toward a repeatable 10+ experiments / month cadence
